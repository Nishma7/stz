Carnatic Music

Among the traditional art forms chosen for their kids, Carnatic Music has been the popular choice for the South Indian diaspora. The reasons could be many. Parents themselves may have learned music while growing up and wish to pass on the joy of learning this captivating musical form to their children. Then there are others who look to Carnatic Music as a way to connect with culture and tradition with an ancient art form that thrives, flourishes and nourishes globally even today. Whatever the reasons are, the benefits of learning music are plenty and Carnatic Music even more so.

Lets take a look:

  • Stress Relief
  • Developing Hand Eye Coordination and Motor Skills
  • Sharper Brain Functions
  • Improves Mathematical Skills
  • Improves Cultural Understanding
  • Improves Listening Skills
  • Provides a medium for self-expression
  • Improves Self-Discipline and Perservance
