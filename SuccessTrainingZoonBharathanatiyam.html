Bharathanatiyam

Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance form that originated in Tamil Nadu, a southern state of India. It is one of the oldest and most popular dance styles in India, with a rich cultural heritage that dates back over 2000 years. Bharatanatyam dance form is characterized by its graceful movements, intricate hand gestures, and rhythmic footwork. The dance is performed to classical Indian music, which is accompanied by the melodious voice of a singer and the beat of a percussion instrument. BARATHANATIYAM has a strong spiritual and religious background, and it is often performed in temples and other religious places..

Why you should learn bharatanatyam dance

  • Good eye exercise
  • Increased physical fitness
  • Enhanced sense of balance
  • Chance to learn new languages
  • Improved flexibility & movement
  • Enhanced self expression
  • Connection to Indian culture
  • Base for learning other dance forms.
  • Improved career prospects
  • Increase stamina
  • Improves concentration
